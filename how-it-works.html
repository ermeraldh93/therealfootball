⚽ How It Works

1. Join

Register with your first name, last name, and birth date.

2. Submit Picks

Pick the winner of every World Cup match before kickoff.

3. Picks Lock

Each match locks automatically when that match starts.

4. Earn Points

Every correct pick earns 1 Leaderboard Point.

5. Chase GOAT Points

Earn GOAT Points for bold underdog and Lone Wolf picks.

6. Win

The player with the most Leaderboard Points at the end wins the jersey of the winning country.

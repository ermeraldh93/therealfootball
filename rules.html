🏆 Official World Cup Pick'Em Rules

⚽ Leaderboard

Every correct prediction earns 1 Leaderboard Point.

The participant with the most Leaderboard Points at the end of the tournament wins the grand prize.

🐐 GOAT Tracker

GOAT Points are separate from the main competition and reward bold predictions.

🐐 Underdog Winner

If a winning team was picked by 25% or fewer participants:

+1 GOAT Point

🐺 Lone Wolf Winner

If only one participant picked the winning team:

+2 GOAT Points

🔥 Can You Earn Both?

Yes.

If you are the only person who picked a winning underdog:

  • 🏆 +1 Leaderboard Point
  • 🐐 +1 Underdog GOAT Point
  • 🐺 +2 Lone Wolf GOAT Points

Total: 1 Leaderboard Point + 3 GOAT Points

🔒 Pick Lock Rules

Picks lock automatically when each match begins.

No changes can be made after kickoff.

🏆 Grand Prize

The participant with the most Leaderboard Points at the end of the tournament wins the jersey of the 2026 FIFA World Cup Champion. 🐐 points will be used as a tiebreaker. If 2 or more participants somehow end up with the same ''Leaderboard score'', participant with the most 🐐🐐🐐s will be the winner.
